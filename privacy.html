Privacy Policy

Last Updated: June 25, 2026

QuickResizer is developed and maintained by Saurabh Kumar Sharma. This Privacy Policy explains how the app uses permissions and protects your information.

Information We Collect

The app does not require an account and does not intentionally collect personally identifiable information.

Permissions

  • Photos – import, edit, compress, resize and save images.
  • Files – import and export PDF documents.
  • Camera – scan documents and QR codes.
  • NFC – only when you use NFC features.

Processing

Most image and PDF processing is performed locally on your device.

Advertising

The free version may display Google AdMob advertisements. Premium removes ads.

Subscriptions

Purchases are securely processed by Apple. We never receive your payment information.

Analytics

The app may use Firebase Analytics and Firebase Crashlytics to improve stability and performance.

Children's Privacy

The app is not directed to children under 13.

Changes

This policy may be updated periodically.
