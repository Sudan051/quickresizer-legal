Privacy Policy

Last Updated: June 28, 2026

Resizer Tools is developed and maintained by Saurabh Kumar Sharma. This Privacy Policy explains what information the app uses, how it is processed, and how your privacy is protected.

Information We Collect

Resizer Tools does not require users to create an account. The app does not intentionally collect personally identifiable information such as your name, email address, or phone number unless you voluntarily contact us for support.

Permissions

  • Photos — Used to import, edit, resize, compress and save images.
  • Files — Used to import, create and export PDF documents.
  • Camera — Used for document scanning and QR code scanning.
  • NFC — Used only when you choose to use NFC features.

Local Processing

Most image processing, PDF processing and QR operations are performed locally on your device whenever possible.

Advertising

The free version of Resizer Tools may display advertisements provided by Google AdMob. Purchasing Premium removes advertisements.

Subscriptions

Premium subscriptions and lifetime purchases are securely processed by Apple's App Store using StoreKit. We never receive or store your payment card information.

Analytics & Diagnostics

To improve app quality and stability, Resizer Tools may use Firebase Analytics and Firebase Crashlytics. These services may collect anonymous diagnostic and usage information.

Third-Party Services

Resizer Tools may use trusted third-party services including:

  • Apple In-App Purchases (StoreKit)
  • Google AdMob
  • Firebase Analytics
  • Firebase Crashlytics

These services operate under their own privacy policies.

Children's Privacy

Resizer Tools is not specifically directed toward children. If you believe personal information has been provided by a child, please contact us so appropriate action can be taken.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be published on this page with an updated revision date.
